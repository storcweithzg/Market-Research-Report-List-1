<p><p>Sealless magnetic drive pumps are designed to eliminate leakage and enhance efficiency, utilizing magnetic coupling to transfer torque without direct contact. The market can be segmented into stationary shaft and rotating shaft types. Stationary shaft pumps have a fixed motor with a magnetic drive assembly for the impeller, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, rotating shaft pumps feature a rotating motor, offering flexibility and ease of maintenance, commonly used in lower-pressure scenarios. Both types provide reliable performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
