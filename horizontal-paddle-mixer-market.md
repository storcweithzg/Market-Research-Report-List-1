<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Discontinuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal paddle mixer market can be categorized into continuous and discontinuous types. Continuous horizontal paddle mixers operate in a steady flow, allowing for uninterrupted mixing of bulk materials, making them ideal for large-scale production processes. In contrast, discontinuous mixers work in batches, where materials are mixed sequentially, providing flexibility for varying product formulations and smaller production volumes. Both types serve diverse industries, including food, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals, each catering to specific operational needs and production requirements.</p></p>
