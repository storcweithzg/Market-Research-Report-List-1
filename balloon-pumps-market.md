<p><p>The balloon pumps market is segmented into two main types: continuous rate type and variable rate type. Continuous rate balloon pumps deliver a steady inflation and deflation cycle, providing consistent support for patients with cardiovascular conditions. In contrast, variable rate balloon pumps allow for adjustable inflation speeds and pressures, tailoring the assistance to the patient's specific needs and physiological responses. Both types are crucial in enhancing cardiac output and stabilizing hemodynamics during critical medical situations.</p></p>
