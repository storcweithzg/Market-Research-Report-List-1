<p><strong>The Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) machinery market is divided into two main types: inline AOI equipment and offline AOI equipment. Inline AOI equipment integrates directly into the manufacturing process, allowing real-time inspection of PCBs during production, which enhances efficiency and reduces cycle times. Offline AOI equipment, on the other hand, operates independently from the production line and is used for batch inspections of assembled PCBs, providing flexibility for more thorough quality control without disrupting ongoing manufacturing activities.</p></p>
