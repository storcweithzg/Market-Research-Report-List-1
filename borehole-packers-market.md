<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Inflatable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Borehole packers are essential tools used in geotechnical and environmental applications to isolate sections of boreholes for testing or sealing. The market consists of two primary types: mechanical packers, which utilize a rigid design often made of metal to create a seal using mechanical means, and inflatable packers, which use an inflatable bladder to form a seal when pressurized. Each type serves unique purposes, with mechanical packers typically favored for durability and inflatable packers for versatility in varying borehole conditions.</p></p>
