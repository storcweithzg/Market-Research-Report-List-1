<p><p>The Neuro Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) market is primarily categorized into two types: Open MRI Systems and Closed MRI Systems. Open MRI Systems offer a wider, more patient-friendly design, allowing individuals who may experience claustrophobia to undergo scans comfortably. Conversely, Closed MRI Systems feature a conventional tube-like structure, typically providing higher-resolution images and faster scan times. The choice between these systems often depends on patient needs, imaging requirements, and the clinical setting where they are utilized.</p></p>
