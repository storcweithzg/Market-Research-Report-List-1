<p><strong>The Volume Bragg Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RBG</li><li>TBG</li><li>CBG</li><li>BNF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Volume Bragg Grating market comprises various types: </p><p>1. **RBG (Reflection Bragg Grating)** utilizes reflected wavelengths to filter and route light in optical devices.</p><p>2. **TBG (Transmission Bragg Grating)** allows specific wavelengths to pass through, enhancing signal processing in communication systems.</p><p>3. **CBG (Coupled Bragg Grating)** integrates both reflection and transmission properties, optimizing interaction between light and materials.</p><p>4. **BNF (Birefringent Neutral Filter)** employs polarization effects to selectively filter light, serving applications in advanced optical setups. Each type enhances optical performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
