<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrodialysis cells market is primarily segmented into continuous and batch electrodialysis systems. Continuous electrodialysis involves a steady flow of feed solutions, allowing for constant ion separation and purification, making it suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes treat fixed volumes of solution in distinct cycles, making it ideal for smaller operations or specific experiments. Both methods leverage ion-exchange membranes to separate ions, but their operational efficiencies and applications differ based on process requirements.</p></p>
