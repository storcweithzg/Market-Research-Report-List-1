<p><p>The herpes simplex virus testing kits market encompasses several types. Antibody/antigen-based kits detect antibodies or viral proteins, providing evidence of infection. Viral culture test kits involve growing the virus from a sample, helping to confirm active infections. Nucleic acid amplification-based kits, such as PCR tests, identify the virus's genetic material, offering highly sensitive and specific results. Each testing type serves distinct clinical needs, from diagnosing active infections to determining past exposure to the herpes simplex virus.</p></p>
