<p><p>The medical antifungal agent market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Azoles**: These inhibit fungal cell membrane synthesis, commonly used for various infections.</p><p>2. **Echinocandins**: Target fungal cell walls, primarily for invasive fungal infections.</p><p>3. **Polyenes**: Bind to fungal cell membranes, effective against systemic infections.</p><p>4. **Allylamines**: Interfere with fungal sterol synthesis, used for skin and nail infections.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category encompasses emerging treatments and alternative agents with antifungal properties.</p></p>
