<p><p>The wall-mounted ventilator market comprises two main types: one-way flow and bidirectional flow ventilators. One-way flow ventilators are designed to either supply fresh air or exhaust stale air in a controlled manner, enhancing indoor air quality. In contrast, bidirectional flow ventilators facilitate simultaneous intake and exhaust of air, promoting balanced ventilation and better air circulation. Both types serve distinct purposes in various settings, contributing to improved comfort and health in residential and commercial environments.</p></p>
