<p><p>The Laser Doppler Flowmeter market comprises two main types: non-invasive perfusion monitoring and invasive microcirculation evaluations. Non-invasive perfusion monitoring utilizes laser technology to assess blood flow and tissue perfusion without requiring skin penetration, making it suitable for routine clinical assessments. In contrast, invasive microcirculation evaluations involve the insertion of a probe to precisely measure blood flow in microvascular systems, offering detailed insights into localized circulation issues. These technologies cater to diverse clinical needs, enhancing patient care and diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
