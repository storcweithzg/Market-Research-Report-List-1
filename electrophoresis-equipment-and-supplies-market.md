Gel electrophoresis involves separating biomolecules using a gel matrix, commonly for DNA and protein analysis. Capillary electrophoresis utilizes narrow capillaries for enhanced resolution and faster results, often applied in genomics and proteomics. Electrophoresis accessories include power supplies, gels, and staining kits, essential for the efficient and precise execution of electrophoresis techniques across research, clinical, and industrial applications.</p></p>
