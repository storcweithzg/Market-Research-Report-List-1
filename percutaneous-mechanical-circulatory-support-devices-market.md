<p><ul><li>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation Device</li><li>Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Device</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Percutaneous Mechanical Circulatory Support Devices market comprises three primary types: Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) devices, Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs), and others. ECMO devices provide both cardiac and respiratory support by oxygenating blood outside the body, useful in severe respiratory or cardiac failure. Extracorporeal VADs assist the heart by improving blood flow in patients with heart failure. The "Others" category includes specialized devices that enhance cardiac performance, targeting various circulatory support needs in critically ill patients.</p></p>
