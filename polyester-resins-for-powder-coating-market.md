<p><p>Polyester resins for powder coating are categorized by their curing methods. Epoxy resin cure offers strong adhesion and chemical resistance, suitable for a variety of applications. TGIC (Triglycidyl Isocyanurate) cure enhances UV stability and gloss, ideal for outdoor uses. HAA (Hexamethoxy Melamine) cure provides excellent durability and chemical resistance, often used in high-performance coatings. Isocyanate cure combines flexibility and hardness, making it suitable for diverse applications. Other methods include hybrid or specialty resins, addressing specific performance requirements.</p></p>
