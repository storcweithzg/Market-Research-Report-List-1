<p><p>The chemical metering pumps market encompasses three primary types: electronically controlled, air control, and insulation type. Electronically controlled pumps offer precise flow rates and easy programming for accurate dispensing. Air control type pumps utilize pneumatic mechanisms, making them suitable for hazardous environments where electricity may pose risks. Insulation type pumps are designed to handle temperature-sensitive applications, ensuring that chemicals remain stable during transfer. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and safety in chemical handling processes.</p></p>
