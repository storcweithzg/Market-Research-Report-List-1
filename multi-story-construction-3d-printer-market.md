<p><p>The multi-story construction 3D printer market comprises primarily two types: gantry systems and robotic arms. Gantry systems utilize a stationary frame that moves along a predefined path, enabling large-scale printing with precision and stability, ideal for constructing entire buildings. In contrast, robotic arms provide flexibility and adaptability, allowing intricate designs and complex geometries to be produced more efficiently. Both technologies enhance construction efficiency, reduce labor costs, and streamline the building process in the rapidly evolving construction industry.</p></p>
