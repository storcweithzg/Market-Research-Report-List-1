<p><ul><li>Flame Melting Method</li><li>Flux Method</li><li>Hydrothermal Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic ruby market includes three primary types: Flame Melting, Flux Method, and Hydrothermal Method. Flame Melting involves intense heat to combine aluminum oxide, resulting in high-purity rubies. The Flux Method employs a chemical flux to dissolve aluminum oxide and crystallize ruby, producing larger stones. The Hydrothermal Method mimics natural geological processes, using high temperature and pressure to grow rubies in a solution, yielding high-quality gems. Each method influences quality, cost, and market demand in the synthetic ruby industry.</p></p>
