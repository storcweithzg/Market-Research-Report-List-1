<p><ul><li>2D Gesture Recognition</li><li>3D Gesture Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gesture recognition market is primarily divided into 2D and 3D gesture recognition. 2D gesture recognition involves interpreting movements captured on flat surfaces, often using touchscreens or 2D cameras, common in smartphones and tablets. In contrast, 3D gesture recognition utilizes depth sensors or specialized cameras to detect movements in three-dimensional space, enabling more complex interactions, such as in virtual reality or gaming applications. Both types enhance user experience by enabling intuitive control and communication through natural movements.</p></p>
