<p><p>The 3D LiDAR sensor market is primarily categorized into Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed optics and electronic components to provide compact, lightweight, and durable solutions, making it ideal for automotive and mobile applications. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture high-resolution 3D data over larger areas. This type offers extensive range and detail, commonly used in mapping, surveying, and autonomous vehicle initiatives, offering versatility across various industries.</p></p>
