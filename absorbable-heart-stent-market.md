<p><p>The absorbable heart stent market is primarily categorized into two types: polymer absorbable heart stents and metal absorbable heart stents. Polymer absorbable stents are made of biocompatible materials that dissolve over time, reducing long-term risks while maintaining scaffold support for initial healing. Metal absorbable stents, on the other hand, utilize bioresorbable metals that gradually break down within the body, offering structural support before being absorbed. Both types aim to minimize complications associated with permanent stents, promoting improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
