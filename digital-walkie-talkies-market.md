<p><p>Digital walkie-talkies are available in two primary types: VHF (Very High Frequency) and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). VHF types operate between 30 to 300 MHz, making them ideal for outdoor use, providing longer range but less effectiveness in dense environments. UHF types, operating between 300 to 3,000 MHz, offer better performance in urban settings and through obstacles, making them suitable for professional communication in buildings and crowded areas. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing communication efficiency.</p></p>
