<p><p>The sourcing software market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based sourcing software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, lower initial costs, and automatic updates. Businesses choose between these types based on their specific needs for control, flexibility, and resource management.</p></p>
