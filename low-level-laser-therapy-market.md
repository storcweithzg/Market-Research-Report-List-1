<p><p>The Low Level Laser Therapy (LLLT) market is categorized into two main types: Continuous Laser and Pulse Laser. Continuous Laser systems emit a steady beam of light, providing consistent energy delivery for therapeutic effects, making them suitable for chronic pain and inflammation treatment. In contrast, Pulse Laser devices emit light in intermittent bursts, enhancing tissue absorption and promoting faster healing for acute injuries. Both types are effective in various medical applications, including pain management and tissue regeneration.</p></p>
