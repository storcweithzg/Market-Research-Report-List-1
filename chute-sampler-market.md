<p><ul><li>Gravity Chute Sampler</li><li>Sloped Gravity Chute Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chute samplers are essential tools for obtaining representative samples from bulk materials in various industries. The Gravity Chute Sampler utilizes gravity to collect samples by directing the flow of material through a chute, ensuring uniform sampling. In contrast, the Sloped Gravity Chute Sampler features a sloped design that enhances material flow, facilitating more effective sampling from moving streams. Both types ensure accurate representation of the bulk material composition, playing a crucial role in quality control and analysis.</p></p>
