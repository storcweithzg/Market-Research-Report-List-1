<p><p>The systemic sclerosis treatments market is categorized into two primary types: Limited Cutaneous Systemic Sclerosis (lcSSc) and Diffuse Cutaneous Systemic Sclerosis (dcSSc). Limited cutaneous systemic sclerosis primarily affects the skin and is generally associated with a better prognosis, leading to therapies focused on managing symptoms and improving quality of life.
