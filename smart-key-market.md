<p><p>The Smart Key Market is divided into two primary types: Capacitive Sensor Technology and Infra-Red Sensor Technology. Capacitive sensors detect the electrical properties of the user's touch or proximity, allowing for keyless entry and ignition systems that enhance convenience and security. Infra-Red sensors use emitted light waves to detect objects or users, often found in remote keyless systems. Both technologies aim to provide driver convenience, streamline access, and improve vehicle safety, catering to the growing demand for advanced automotive solutions.</p></p>
