<p><p>Confocal Laser Scanning Microscope (CLSM) is a sophisticated imaging technique used to obtain high-resolution, 3D images of biological specimens. The market for CLSM is primarily divided into two types: single-photon CLSM and multiphoton CLSM. Single-photon CLSM utilizes a single laser wavelength to focus on a specimen, enabling quick imaging, while multiphoton CLSM employs multiple photons, allowing deeper tissue penetration and reduced photodamage, making it ideal for live-cell imaging. Both technologies find extensive applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
