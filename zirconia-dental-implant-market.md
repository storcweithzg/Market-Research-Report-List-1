<p><p>The zirconia dental implant market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Endosteal Implants**: These are the most common type, placed directly into the jawbone. Made entirely of zirconia, they provide strong support for prosthetic teeth.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Subperiosteal Implants**: Positioned under the gum but above the jawbone, these implants are suitable for patients lacking adequate bone height and provide stability for dentures.</p><p>3. **Other Implants**: This category includes various specialized designs and configurations, addressing unique patient needs and anatomical considerations.</p></p>
