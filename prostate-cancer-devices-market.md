External beam radiation therapy (EBRT) devices deliver targeted radiation to destroy cancer cells. Brachytherapy involves implanting radioactive seeds directly within the prostate. High-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) devices use ultrasound waves to heat and destroy cancer tissue non-invasively. Cryotherapy devices freeze cancer cells, effectively killing them. Together, these innovative devices support diverse strategies for diagnosing and treating prostate cancer.</p></p>
