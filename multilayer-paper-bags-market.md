<p><p>The multilayer paper bags market encompasses several types, each serving specific packaging needs. Sewn open mouth bags are durable and ideal for bulk materials. Pasted open mouth bags feature a glued opening for easy filling. Pasted valve bags have a one-way valve to facilitate air escape, suitable for powders. Pinch bottom bags allow for quick sealing and are often used for food products. Self-opening satchel bags, designed for easy access and filling, are commonly used in retail settings.</p></p>
