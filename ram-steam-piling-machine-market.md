<p><p>The Ram Steam Piling Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Acting Steam Hammers and Double Acting Steam Hammers. Single Acting Steam Hammers utilize steam pressure to drive the ram downward, making them suitable for lighter piles and less demanding applications. In contrast, Double Acting Steam Hammers employ steam pressure for both upward and downward strokes, providing increased efficiency and power, ideal for heavy-duty piling tasks. Both types are essential in construction for efficient and effective pile driving operations.</p></p>
