<p><p>The tank gauge market consists of various types catering to different storage needs. Pressure tanks are designed to hold gases or liquids under pressure, enabling precise monitoring. Stationary tanks are fixed installations used for bulk storage of liquids. Transport tankers are mobile solutions for the transportation of liquids and gases, often equipped with advanced gauging systems. Cryogenic or liquefied gas tanks are specialized for maintaining low temperatures for gases like nitrogen or oxygen, requiring specific gauges to ensure safety and efficiency.</p></p>
