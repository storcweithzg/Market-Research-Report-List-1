<p><ul><li>LED</li><li>Halogen</li><li>Fluorescent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The examination lamps market is categorized into three main types: LED, halogen, and fluorescent. LED lamps are energy-efficient, providing bright illumination with low heat output and a long lifespan, making them ideal for detailed examinations. Halogen lamps offer focused light and a higher color temperature, enhancing visibility in clinical settings but consume more energy. Fluorescent lamps are cost-effective and provide even lighting but may lack the brightness and clarity required for precision tasks. Each type serves specific needs in medical environments.</p></p>
