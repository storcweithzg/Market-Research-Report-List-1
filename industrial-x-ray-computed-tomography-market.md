<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography (CT) market comprises two primary types: Line Beam Scanning and Cone Beam Scanning. Line Beam Scanning utilizes a linear X-ray beam that moves across the object, providing high-resolution images suitable for detailed inspections of complex geometries. In contrast, Cone Beam Scanning employs a cone-shaped X-ray beam to capture volumetric data in a single rotation, offering faster imaging and a broader field of view, making it ideal for large-scale components and real-time analysis.</p></p>
