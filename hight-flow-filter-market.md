<p><p>High flow filters are designed to efficiently remove contaminants from liquids and gases, and they come in various types based on the membrane material. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) filters are known for their chemical resistance and low surface tension, making them ideal for aggressive solvents. PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride) filters offer excellent mechanical strength and thermal stability, suitable for harsh environments. Nylon filters provide high flow rates and are commonly used in water purification. Other materials may include polypropylene or cellulose, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
