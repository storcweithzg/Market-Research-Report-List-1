Grade 304 offers good corrosion resistance and is commonly used in general applications. Grade 316, enriched with molybdenum, provides superior resistance to pitting and is ideal for harsh environments, such as chemical processing. Other types may include high-strength or high-temperature alloys catering to specific industrial needs, enabling applications across diverse sectors like pharmaceuticals, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
