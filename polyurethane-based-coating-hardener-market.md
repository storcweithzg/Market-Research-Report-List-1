<p><p>The polyurethane-based coating hardener market is categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based hardeners utilize water as a primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions, easier cleanup, and eco-friendliness, making them popular for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based hardeners use organic solvents, providing superior durability and adhesion, making them suitable for demanding environments but with higher VOC levels. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing environmental concerns with performance requirements.</p></p>
