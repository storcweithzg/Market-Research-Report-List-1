<p><p>Telecom obstruction lighting systems are categorized into three intensity levels based on the height and location of telecommunications structures. Low-intensity lights are used for towers under 200 feet, ensuring visibility to aircraft at lower altitudes. Medium-intensity lights, suitable for towers between 200 and 500 feet, provide enhanced visibility for higher air traffic. High-intensity lights are designed for structures exceeding 500 feet, offering maximum visibility to avoid collisions. Each type is crucial for aviation safety while accommodating various tower heights.</p></p>
