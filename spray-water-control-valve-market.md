<p><strong>The Spray Water Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Way Type</li><li>3-Way Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spray Water Control Valve market comprises primarily two types: 2-Way and 3-Way valves. The 2-Way type allows flow in a single direction, effectively controlling the on/off operation of water supply to spray systems, making it ideal for straightforward applications. In contrast, the 3-Way type facilitates directional flow control, enabling mixing or diverting functions, which is beneficial in complex spray setups. Both types are essential for optimizing water usage and enhancing operational efficiency in various industrial and agricultural applications.</p></p>
