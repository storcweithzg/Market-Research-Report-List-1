<p><strong>The Bus Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFP</li><li>NMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus battery market primarily consists of two key types: Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) and Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC). LFP batteries are known for their safety, thermal stability, and long cycle life, making them ideal for urban buses that prioritize longevity and reliability. In contrast, NMC batteries offer higher energy density and efficiency, suited for longer-range applications and rapid charging needs. Both types are crucial in the transition to electric buses, balancing performance, safety, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
