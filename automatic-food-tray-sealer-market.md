<p><p>The Automatic Food Tray Sealer market is categorized into two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully Automatic Food Tray Sealers. In contrast, Fully Automatic Food Tray Sealers perform all sealing processes autonomously, enhancing efficiency and production speed, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturers.
