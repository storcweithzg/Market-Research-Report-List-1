<p><p>The Vacuum Heat Treatment Furnace market includes three primary types: single chamber, double chamber, and multi-chamber furnaces. A single chamber furnace operates in one sealed space, providing simplicity and efficiency for various heat treatment processes. The double chamber furnace separates processes, allowing for better control and reduced contamination between stages. Multi-chamber furnaces offer versatility with multiple chambers for different treatments, enabling higher productivity and the simultaneous processing of varying materials, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
