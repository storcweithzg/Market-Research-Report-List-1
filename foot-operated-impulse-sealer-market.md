<p><strong>The Foot Operated Impulse Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Impulse</li><li>Double Impulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foot-operated impulse sealer market is divided into two main types: single impulse and double impulse sealers. Single impulse sealers create a seal with one heat cycle, suitable for low-volume packaging and thinner materials. In contrast, double impulse sealers apply heat from both top and bottom sealing jaws, ensuring a stronger seal, making them ideal for thicker materials and higher-volume operations. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing productivity and efficiency in sealing processes.</p></p>
