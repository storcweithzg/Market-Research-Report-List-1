<p><p>Carded blister packaging is a common method for presenting products in a protective, visually appealing manner. The market is primarily divided into two types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating and shaping plastic sheets over a mold, which allows for intricate designs and customizations. Cold forming, on the other hand, uses pressure to shape materials without heat, offering enhanced durability and moisture protection. Both types are widely used in pharmaceuticals, electronics, and consumer goods to ensure product safety and visibility.</p></p>
